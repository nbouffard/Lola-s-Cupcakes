Lola's Cupcakes!

About Me

Welcome everyone to my page! My name is Gloria but my beloved grandchildren call me Lola! For so many years it has been my dream to bring people joy in the form of cupcakes. I am a passionate self taught baker living in the heart of London. I have two wonderful daughters and am blessed with three granddaughters. For what feels like many eons ago, I was in a traditional 9-5 job living to work until my health had other plans. Since then I have found my life's true passion.... you guessed it cupcakes!
